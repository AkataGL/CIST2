The Tour 250

Go for a ride, come back in a month. This is the idea behind Pedal Mart's line of touring bikes. The Tour 250 is a classic example of a rugged touring bike designed for days, weeks, and even months of continuous use. It's loaded with features for the serious cyclist, including reinforced braze-ons for pannier racks. It's also easy on your body with a suspension seat post, spring saddle, and adjustable high-rise seat stem, perfect for long tours and long days. All parts are engineered to the highest quality. Quick release levers for all major parts make roadside repairs a breeze.
