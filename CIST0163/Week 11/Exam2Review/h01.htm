Hole 1

Men

  • Par: 5
  • Distance: 475 yds
  • Handicap: 7

Women

  • Par: 5
  • Distance: 401 yds
  • Handicap: 7

A perfect hole to begin your day at Willet Creek. Long hitters have a reasonable chance at birdie. The hole plays slightly uphill. When the wind is blowing in, it can be an especially challenging hole to par. Two good shots will leave a short approach shot to an elevated green.
