Hole 4

Men

  • Par: 4
  • Distance: 325 yds
  • Handicap: 1

Women

  • Par: 4
  • Distance: 280 yds
  • Handicap: 1

This dogleg left tempts the golfer to go for the green. The smart play is approach the green from the right fairway, and hit your approach shot on to the right side of the green. Although a short hole, a par is well earned.
